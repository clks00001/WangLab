About Lab

Research on the Mechanism of Growth Factors/Stem Cells and Myocardial and Neural Regeneration In the research of elderly regenerative medicine, the use of photogenetics to promote elderly rehabilitation and prolong lifespan has expanded new methods for phototherapy in the field of elderly regenerative medicine, and elucidated the role of GnRH frequency in regulating aging and lifespan. Clarified the effects and mechanisms of diet targeting the hypothalamus and improving metabolism, muscle, and other related factors in elderly regeneration. Research on Growth Factors and Metabolism and Systemic Aging In the field of spinal cord regenerative medicine, a three-dimensional and diversified system for spinal cord injury rehabilitation based on neural repair and reactivation of body activity has been constructed, significantly improving the speed of spinal cord injury regeneration. Biomaterials and nerve regeneration In the field of cardiac regenerative medicine, we have conducted research on the application and mechanism of growth factors and scaffold biomaterials in the field of cardiac rehabilitation medicine, utilizing zebrafish ECM scaffolds combined with exercise to promote myocardial infarction injury regeneration.
